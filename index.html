    About Me

    Welcome to My Journey of Quality, Adventure, and Pixels!

    Hello everyone! I'm Justyna Spital, a passionate software engineer and an avid traveler. While my career has been focused on supplier quality assurance, I've recently embarked on a new adventure in software development. I’ll share my unique blend of experiences in quality management, food safety, and my budding journey in programming, sprinkled with a dash of gaming fun.

    Who Am I?

    I have over a 6 years of experience in the food industry, ensuring top-notch quality and safety standards. A year ago, I decided to dive into the world of software development. Since then, I’ve been coding, learning new technologies, and finding ways to integrate my quality assurance expertise into software engineering. I've traveled to over 18 countries, and often find myself analyzing the quality of my experiences, both food safety and code-related. Oh, and did I mention? I’m also an enthusiastic gamer, finding joy and relaxation in exploring virtual worlds and honing my strategic skills.

    My motto? "Quality isn’t expensive, it’s priceless. But bad quality? That’ll cost you!"

    As I navigate through the realms of supplier quality assurance and software development, I believe in maintaining a work-life balance that’s as smooth as my code and as fulfilling as a perfect meal. My advice to aspiring professionals: “Travel often. The world is the best classroom, and sometimes, the best quality checks are done with a passport in hand.” And when you need a break, don’t forget to fire up your favorite game—it’s not just entertainment; it's a training ground for problem-solving and teamwork. Thank you for joining me as I explore the fascinating intersections of quality assurance, food safety, software development, and gaming!
